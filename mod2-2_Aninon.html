Avengers: Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" is a 2019 superhero film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The film follows the Avengers as they attempt to reverse the devastating effects of Thanos' snap, which wiped out half of all life. They embark on a time-travel mission to retrieve the Infinity Stones and save the universe, resulting in an epic battle against Thanos. The film is a culmination of the MCU's Phase Three.
